# Vitalis — Your Personal Health AI

> Tell it what you ate. It tracks your nutrition, detects deficiencies, builds your grocery list, and books a doctor appointment when something looks serious. Automatically.

---

## Try It Now

**[t.me/vitalis_health_ai_bot](https://t.me/vitalis_health_ai_bot)**

1. Click the link above
2. Press **Start**
3. Send a voice note or text saying what you ate
4. That's it

No app to download. No account to create. Just talk to it.

---

## What It Does

- Voice or text — send a voice note or just type what you ate
- Instant nutrition breakdown — calories, protein, carbs, fat, iron, vitamin D, B12, calcium, and a health score
- Deficiency detection — tracks your nutrition across the full week and flags what is critically low
- Grocery list — automatically suggests what to buy to fix your gaps
- Doctor appointment — if your pattern looks serious, it books an appointment on Google Calendar without being asked

---

## Commands

| Command | What it does |
|---|---|
| Send any text | Log and analyse a meal |
| Send a voice note | Transcribe and analyse a meal |
| `/summary` | Your 7-day nutrition report |
| `/grocery` | Your personalised weekly grocery list |
| `/setgoals` | Update your health goals |
